# Visual Direction

## UX Constraint

Calm does not mean sparse.

- Preserve all decision-critical information.
- Reduce noise, not clarity.
- Prioritize readability over aesthetic minimalism.

Every page should answer:
- Where am I?
- Why is this worth considering?
- Who is responsible for the stay?
- What can I do next?
- What happens if I request this booking?

## Visual Principles

Restraint:
- Use fewer accents, but make them purposeful.
- Prefer clear spacing and strong hierarchy over decorative dividers.

Specificity:
- Use market, collection, Provider, and stay details to make pages feel grounded.
- Avoid generic travel language and generic scenery cues.

Trust:
- Keep price, availability, reviews, Provider identity, and booking state close to the action.
- Use badges only for real platform signals.

Warmth:
- Use human copy, natural image direction, and generous line height.
- Avoid sterile minimalism.

## Theme Tokens

Local fallback theme:

| Token | Value | Brand role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Primary | `#2f6f5e` | cedar green, used for actions and selected states |
| Primary dark | `#25564a` | hover and active states |
| Primary light | `#3f8a72` | focus and secondary emphasis |
| Ink | `#1f2926` | headings and primary text |
| Soft ground | `#f6f8f5` | page background |
| Line | `#d9ded8` | quiet boundaries |
| Clay accent | `#a35f45` | rare editorial accent |
| Coast accent | `#3d7485` | rare market accent |

Rules:
- Do not return to purple or purple-blue as the dominant brand color.
- Do not build one-note green pages.
- Use accents sparingly and only to clarify market, collection, or trust context.
- Buttons and cards should keep border radius at `8px` or less.
- Letter spacing stays `0`.

Typography:
- Use the existing system sans stack.
- Prioritize readable sizes and line heights over personality fonts.
- Do not scale font size with viewport width.

## Image Roles

Hero:
- emotional positioning

Listings:
- clear, decision-oriented

Sections:
- supportive

Hosts:
- trust-building

## Image Constraints

- natural light
- real environments
- no staged stock feel
- consistent tone
- no literal cultural motifs as decoration
- no over-filtered luxury treatment

Placeholder rule:
Use neutral placeholders until final assets exist. Placeholder media must protect the layout,
aspect ratios, and content hierarchy without pretending to be real inventory.
