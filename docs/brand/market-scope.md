# Market Scope

## Initial Markets

### Tier 1 (Launch)

- Nikko
- Kamakura

### Tier 2

- Hakone
- Chiba: Narita or Ubara

## Market Roles

### Nikko

- Nature and heritage
- Slow, immersive stays
- Best for Travelers seeking quiet, depth, and time outdoors

### Kamakura

- Coastal and accessible
- Culture and lifestyle
- Best for Travelers seeking an easier entry point with daily-life texture

### Hakone

- Onsen and retreat
- Premium calm
- Best for Travelers seeking restorative stays and stronger comfort expectations

### Chiba

Narita:
- Arrival/departure calm stays
- Best for Travelers who want a softer first or last night

Ubara:
- Hidden coastal discovery
- Best for Travelers seeking quieter coastline and fewer crowds

## Geographic Curation Rule

- Each market must reflect a core pillar.
- Do not expand into generic, high-volume markets.
- Quality is more important than coverage.

Expansion test:
Before adding a market, answer:
- What role does this market play that the current set does not?
- Which collection does it strengthen?
- Can MyYado maintain a consistent quality bar there?
- Are there enough reviewed Listings to avoid a thin launch?
